    Customer management made SIMPLER

    As we shift our system to be digitized we strive to make our data more central, easily accessible to you.

    With the implementation of the database and server we guaranteee faster execution of queries and less work needed. Want to access a customers all time data? get it within seconds. Made an error in a meter reading? Easy correction of data is now simpler and as a bonus the customer is notified upon correction.

    Productivity, service delivery and efficiency have been bumped up 100% with this shift making work easier.

    Access to consumer data and other features for easier consumer management made available

    Customer management couldn't be more simpler

    Easy management off consumers could not be any more easier you can now perform complex operations such as messaging users with the click of a button. You can also notify users when there are disruptions in water supply

    Communication with customers made easier

    Users are sent messages on every month notifying them of their bills and updates when payments are made and when to expect disruptions in water supply and other notification that may concern them.

    Analysis of consumer data made less tedious

    Analyzing consumer data such as consumption, payments made easier and with the use of visual aids makes it easier interpretation. Analysis can be done monthly, quarterly or annually.
